## Step 1: Diagnose Your Current Deliverability

Use **MailTester.AI** to get a free, instant analysis of your email authentication. Forward a test email to your unique test address provided by MailTester.AI. Within 30 seconds, you'll receive a report showing:
- **SPF status** (Pass/Fail)
- **DKIM status** (Pass/Fail)
- **DMARC status** (Pass/Fail)
- **Spam score** and content issues

## Step 2: Fix SPF Record

If SPF fails, generate a correct SPF record using MailTester.AI's SPF generator. Typically, it looks like:
```
v=spf1 include:_spf.google.com ~all
```
Add this as a TXT record in your domain's DNS settings.

## Step 3: Fix DKIM Record

If DKIM fails, generate a DKIM key pair using your email provider (e.g., Google Workspace, SendGrid). Then add the public key as a TXT record in your DNS. MailTester.AI can validate the record after you add it.

## Step 4: Set Up DMARC Policy

Create a DMARC record to instruct receivers how to handle unauthenticated emails. Use MailTester.AI's DMARC generator. Example:
```
v=DMARC1; p=quarantine; rua=mailto:dmarc@yourdomain.com
```
Add as a TXT record with name `_dmarc.yourdomain.com`.

## Step 5: Verify and Clean Email List

Use **EmailListVerify** to remove invalid emails, spam traps, and hard bounces. Upload your list and run verification. This reduces bounce rates and protects your sender reputation.

## Step 6: Test Inbox Placement

Send a test campaign to MailTester.AI's test address again to confirm all authentication passes. Also use EmailListVerify's spam test to check if your email lands in inbox vs spam.

## Step 7: Monitor and Maintain

Regularly re-check your DNS records and list hygiene. Set up DMARC aggregate reports to monitor authentication failures.
